# Product Overview

## What is this project?

A comprehensive Business Management Dashboard (CRM/ERP-style application) for managing business operations including leads, customers, employees, projects, services, payments, inventory, and reports.

## Key Features

- **Lead Management**: Track and manage sales leads
- **Customer Management**: Maintain customer information and details
- **Employee Management**: Manage team members and their information
- **Project Tracking**: Monitor project progress and status
- **Service Management**: Manage services offered and their details
- **Payment Processing**: Handle payment transactions
- **Inventory Management**: Track inventory levels and items
- **Reports & Analytics**: View business metrics and KPIs
- **User Authentication**: Secure login system
- **Dashboard**: Central hub with KPI cards and key metrics

## Target Users

Business managers, sales teams, and operations staff who need a centralized platform to manage multiple aspects of their business operations.

## Development Context

This is a Lovable project (AI-assisted development platform) that uses modern web technologies for rapid development and iteration.
